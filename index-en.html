Who am I?
A little about Pedro Toscano.

Since I was young, I've always had a strong connection with technology. Video games and computers always fascinated me, and it was this curiosity that led me to the world of exact sciences. For a long time, I searched for what truly inspired me, until I realized that computers were always in front of me as the natural choice.

I haven't officially entered the job market yet, but I've had the opportunity to work on some projects I'm very proud of. One of them was an educational math game developed with college friends. This project showed me how much I was advancing in my development skills and provided a collaborative experience.

I am extremely focused on solving problems and believe that without a challenge, there is no true purpose in work. My greatest motivation is my family and the desire to positively impact the world. I believe my role is to transform the world into a better place through my contributions.
